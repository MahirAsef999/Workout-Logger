About Fitness Haven

Hello! I'm Mahir, a Computer Science major and a junior at Fordham University. I created Fitness Haven as a personal project to combine my interests in tech and fitness.

What inspired me to create this app was my journey of starting to go to the gym. I had recently gotten into the gym and I realized that having a ditigal tracking log helped me track my progress, as I would be able to record my exercises and will not lose it like I would if I had a paper log. I believe that tracking workouts digitally is an essential habit that helps maintain consistency and monitor progress. This app serves as a tool for anyone on a fitness journey, helping you stay motivated and track your improvements.

Fitness Haven is designed to be simple, intuitive, and motivating. I hope it helps you as much as it has helped me in maintaining my workout routine.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a workout?

To log a workout, simply fill in the workout form with the date, exercise type, and any other relevant details like sets, reps, and weight. Click "Log Workout" to save it.

Can I track my progress over time?

Yes! You can view your logged workouts and monitor your progress. The app also shows your most frequent exercises and total workouts logged.

What data do you store about my workouts?

We store your exercise type, weight, sets, reps, date, time, distance, and calories burned. You can also update or delete this information at any time.

How can I update or delete a workout log?

You can edit or delete your logged workouts by clicking the "Edit" or "Delete" button next to each log entry.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is secure. We take your privacy very seriously. Only you can see your workout logs and no one else!
